    Piccadilly Greens is located in the prime location of Dasak. Every part of this township is designed and built with great care.     About Surashtra Estates

    Surashtra Estates aspires to be a brand that reflects quality, trust and excellence in everything it does.

    We pride ourselves in working on a blank slate and building properties from the ground up. Our work goes beyond than just buying and developing land. It is about improving communities – socially, economically, and financially.

    Surashtra Estates is a privately held real estate development and construction firm. We develop by helping other people and other businesses grow. We leverage our extensive real estate experience to create value at every stage of the development process: design, construction, acquisition, entitlement, and sales.
